Threat intelligence

Threat intelligence is a service which gather the worldwide existing threats information to help customers have greater visibility into threats.

My Role
 UX designer
Key Skills
 Competitor study,
Eco-system study,
E2E User flow
Design process
Eco-system study
  • Explore the relationship of the threat intelligence with other features in the product.
  • Consider the overall user experience to provide cross features and products flow.
Competitor study
  • Study competitors' product to see how other vendors provide the Threat intelligence service.
  • Present the study result to the team and discuss the missing point in our Threat intelligence features.
User flow
  • Based on user scenarios and use cases to create the user flow.
  • Consider overall user journey to create in- app and cross apps user flows.
Design goal

Help Security Operation Center (SOC) team to answer the following questions:

  • What is the trending of the cyber attack?
  • Am I affected by any attack?
  • How serious and what’s the impact of the attack?
Design outcome
What is the trending of the cyber attack?
  • Provide up-to-date threat attack reports and sort by laset updated time.
  • While the threat attack reports are worldwide, help customers use filter to narrow down to the scope that they need to take care.
Am I affected by any attack?
  • The summary widget shows how many attacks are under tracking and what attacks might affact customer’s environment.
  • Customers could use the filters to view all possible attack matched result.
How serious and what’s the impact of the attack?
  • In the attack matched summary, customer could know the impact scope and the attack indicators.
  • User could view more detail through the network chart.
